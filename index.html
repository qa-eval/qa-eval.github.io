About EVOUNA

This paper introduces a new task—evaluating QA evaluation (QA-Eval) and creates a corresponding dataset named EVOUNA (EValuating Open qUestion aNswering evAluation). This dataset utilizes human-annotated results to assess the performance of different evaluators, aiming to calculate the correlation between evaluators and human annotations.
